## Slide 2: Executive Summary | 执行摘要

**Title | 标题**: 通过三城聚焦、精准补贴与剥离低效资产，3 年内实现组合 EBIT 稳健转正
**Action Title | 行动主题**: Three Focus Cities + Precision Subsidies + Asset Divestiture = EBIT Breakeven in 3 Years
**Tracker | 追踪**: Executive Summary
**Content | 内容**:

- **挑战本质**：当前 1.35-1.5 亿年亏损是"结构性单位经济错配"，而非市场需求不足。
  **Challenge**: The current annual loss of RMB 135-150M reflects a structural unit economics misalignment—not insufficient market demand.

- **战略重心从"扩张"转向"聚焦"**：放弃全国 10 城齐头并进，在 12-18 个月内优先在泉州、惠州、威海 3 城验证盈利模型（止血优先）。
  **Strategic pivot**: Shift from "nationwide expansion" to "focused prioritization." Abandon the 10-city parallel approach; instead, validate the profitability model in Quanzhou, Huizhou, and Weihai within 12-18 months (止血 = stem the bleeding first).

- **两把核心反转钥匙**：
  **Two key levers for turnaround**:
  1. **补贴纠偏**：从"补单量"切割向"补高价值用户留存"，释放被低效消耗的 80% 补贴资源。
     **Subsidy rebalancing**: Shift from "subsidizing order volume" to "investing in high-value user retention"—unlocking ~80% of subsidy resources currently wasted on inefficiency.
  2. **竞争升维**：利用美团战略防守期，腾出资源直击真正对手（线下超市），强化"30分钟即时履约"降维打击。
     **Competitive escalation**: Capitalize on Meituan's strategic defensiveness to redirect resources toward the real competitor—offline supermarkets—leveraging "30-minute instant fulfillment" as a differentiated attack.

- **财务里程碑**：Year 1 止血验证，Year 2 优化组跟进实现整体组合单季转正（+3,300万），Year 3 实现 10城组合整体盈利。
  **Financial milestones**: Year 1—validate bleed-stopping; Year 2—optimization tier follows, achieving portfolio quarterly breakeven (+RMB 33M); Year 3—full 10-city portfolio profitability.

## Slide 5: Argument 1 (City Playbook) | 论点 1（城市策略）

**Title | 标题**: 规模不是解药，三城路径不可互通复制，必须按城市基因匹配策略
**Action Title | 行动主题**: Scale Is Not the Answer: One Size Does Not Fit All—Each City Requires a Tailored Playbook
**Tracker | 追踪**: 城市策略 | 三城三路径
**Content | 内容**:

- **泉州模式 = 时间积累下的供应链深度**：以 23% 的大超作为核心护城河。
  **Quanzhou model = supply chain depth built over time**: Hypermarket share at 23% serves as the core moat.

- **惠州模式 = 极致密度下的基础设施复用**：以绝对规模（130k 单/天）压平成本。
  **Huizhou model = infrastructure leverage through extreme density**: Absolute scale (130K orders/day) flattens per-unit costs.

- **威海模式 = 品类优化带来的高客单结构**：快消品类主导将 AOV 抬升至 65 元，实现最优 UE。
  **Weihai model = high-AOV structure through category mix optimization**: FMCG-dominant category mix pushes AOV to RMB 65, delivering best-in-class unit economics.

- **启示**：不可在徐州复制泉州的供应链，也不可在赣州强推威海的高客单；必须"因城施策"。
  **Key insight**: Quanzhou's supply chain cannot be replicated in Xuzhou; Weihai's high-AOV playbook cannot be forced onto Ganzhou. Strategy must be "tailored to each city's DNA."
  **Source | 来源**: 城市横截面对比分析 (AX-A2)

## Slide 9: Argument 2 (Subsidy Reallocation) | 论点 2（补贴重新分配）

**Title | 标题**: 核心抓手是纠正补贴错配，从"花钱买单量"转向"投资高留存"
**Action Title | 行动主题**: Fix the Subsidy Mismatch: Stop Buying Volume, Start Investing in Retention
**Tracker | 追踪**: 补贴优化 | 资源再分配
**Content | 内容**:

- **效率鸿沟**：C10 赣州（单均 4.9 元/高留存率 15%）与 C8 威海（单均 3.5 元/高留存率 26%）存在超 2 倍的补贴效率差。
  **Efficiency gap**: C10 Ganzhou (RMB 4.9/order with 15% high retention) vs. C8 Weihai (RMB 3.5/order with 26% high retention)—a >2x subsidy efficiency gap.

- **核心问题**：全盘目前有 80% 的补贴浪费在极低复购、仅对价格敏感的边缘用户身上。
  **Core problem**: Currently, ~80% of subsidies are wasted on price-sensitive, low-repeat edge users with minimal repurchase behavior.

- **策略转向**：KPI 考核需从"单均补贴额"全面切换为"获取/维系单个高价值用户的成本"。
  **Strategic pivot**: KPI framework must shift from "subsidy per order" to "cost to acquire/retain a single high-value user."
